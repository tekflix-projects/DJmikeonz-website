FAQ

Good Questions. Real Answers.

When should I book?

Booking is open anytime — as long as the date is available, it's yours. That said, the sooner the better, especially for busy summer weekends and peak wedding season. Don't wait on a date you love.

How quickly will I hear back after submitting the form?

You'll receive a response within 24 hours. Every event is different, so DJ MIKEONZ will put together a custom quote tailored specifically to your event — no two celebrations are priced the same.

Do you travel outside the DFW Metroplex?

Absolutely. DJ MIKEONZ is available for events beyond the DFW area. Travel details will be factored into your custom quote.

How do you determine your pricing?

Pricing varies depending on several factors — distance, event type, hours, guest count, equipment needed, and any additional services required. Submit the booking form and you'll receive a quote built around your specific event.

Do you take song requests?

Yes. Song requests are always welcome. DJ MIKEONZ works with you ahead of time to understand your must-plays, your do-not-plays, and everything in between — and on the night, he'll read the room and weave it all together seamlessly.

Is there a minimum booking duration?

No minimum — whether it's a short set or an all-night affair, DJ MIKEONZ will show up and deliver regardless of the duration.

Do you provide your own equipment?

Yes. DJ MIKEONZ arrives fully equipped with professional, top-of-the-line gear. You don't need to source or rent anything — just show up and let the music handle the rest.

What happens if there's a technical issue on the day?

Professional preparation means technical issues are rare — but never impossible. DJ MIKEONZ comes equipped with backup gear and redundant systems so the music keeps playing no matter what. In the unlikely event something comes up, it's handled quickly and discreetly. Your guests won't miss a beat.

Can we have a consultation before booking?

Of course. A quick 15-minute consultation is always available before you commit. It's a great way to talk through your vision, ask questions, and make sure the fit is right before anything is locked in.
